Kenya income tax bands for 2026

Kenya taxes income progressively: each band's rate applies only to the income that falls within it, not to your whole salary. Figures below are annual taxable-income thresholds in KES.

Annual income (KES)Rate
0 – 288,00010%
288,000 – 388,00025%
388,000 – 6,000,00030%
6,000,000 – 9,600,00032.5%
Above 9,600,00035%

Notes

  • Personal relief of KES 28,800/year is a credit against tax, so PAYE is nil for the lowest earners.
  • Insurance relief (15% of qualifying premiums, up to KES 60,000/year) may also apply on top of personal relief.
  • This is PAYE income tax only — NSSF, SHIF (2.75% of gross) and the Affordable Housing Levy (1.5%) further reduce take-home pay.
