# What is Dependent Qualification?

Dependent qualification is a term used in logic, mathematics, and computer science to describe a condition or set of conditions that must be met in order for something to be considered true or valid.

In simple terms, a dependent qualification is a requirement that is connected to or dependent on something else. For example, in a logical statement, a dependent qualification might be a clause that must be met in order for the entire statement to be considered true.

In general, dependent qualifications help to clarify and specify the conditions under which something is true or valid, and they are an important part of many logical and mathematical systems.
