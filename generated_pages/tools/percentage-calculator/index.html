Percentage Calculator

How to Use This Calculator

To use the percentage calculator, enter the initial value, the percentage to apply, and select whether you want to calculate the percentage of the initial value or the initial value based on the percentage result. For example, if you want to find 25% of $120, you would enter 120 as the initial value and 25 as the percentage.

Let's say you want to calculate a 15% discount on a $200 purchase: you enter 200 as the initial value and 15 as the percentage, and the calculator will output the discount amount and the final price after the discount.

The Formula Behind It

The formula for calculating percentages is: result = (percentage / 100) * initial value.

The variables are:

  • result: the calculated percentage value
  • percentage: the percentage to apply
  • initial value: the base value.

Practical Examples

Here are a few examples of using the percentage calculator:

  • If you earn $50,000 per year and get a 10% raise, the calculator will show that your new salary is $55,000.
  • A store is having a 20% off sale on a $100 item, and the calculator will output that the discount is $20 and the final price is $80.
  • If a company's stock price increases by 5% from $500 to a new price, the calculator will show that the new price is $525.

Common Questions

What is the difference between percentage and percent?

Percentage and percent are often used interchangeably, but technically percent means "out of 100" and percentage is the result of a calculation.

How do I calculate reverse percentage?

To calculate the initial value based on a percentage result, enter the result and the percentage in the calculator.

Can I use the calculator for negative percentages?

Yes, you can use the calculator to calculate negative percentages, such as a 10% decrease.

How do I calculate a percentage of a percentage?

To calculate a percentage of a percentage, first calculate the first percentage, then use the result as the initial value to calculate the second percentage.

What if I need to calculate multiple percentages?

You can use the calculator to calculate each percentage separately, or calculate the total percentage by adding the individual percentages.
