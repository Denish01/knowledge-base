Common Questions

What is the difference between a fixed-rate and adjustable-rate mortgage?

A fixed-rate mortgage has an interest rate that remains the same for the entire loan term, while an adjustable-rate mortgage has an interest rate that can change over time.

How does the down payment affect the monthly payment?

The down payment reduces the principal loan amount, resulting in a lower monthly payment.

Can I use this calculator for refinancing a mortgage?

Yes, you can use this calculator to determine the monthly payment for a refinanced mortgage by entering the new loan amount, interest rate, and loan term.

What is the impact of the interest rate on the monthly payment?

A higher interest rate results in a higher monthly payment, while a lower interest rate results in a lower monthly payment.

How does the loan term affect the monthly payment?

A longer loan term results in a lower monthly payment, but you will pay more in interest over the life of the loan, while a shorter loan term results in a higher monthly payment, but you will pay less in interest.
